1. First Login

Use the username and temporary password provided on the laptop sticker.

Important: Before typing your password, make sure you are using the correct keyboard layout. Special characters such as @ and # may be different on UK and US keyboards.
Character UK Keyboard US Keyboard
@ Shift + ' Shift + 2
# Key near Enter Shift + 3

2. Change Your Password

After your first login, you will be asked to create a new password.

Password Requirements

  • At least 12 characters
  • Uppercase letter
  • Lowercase letter
  • Number
  • Special character

Avoid Using

  • Your first name
  • Your surname
  • Company name
  • Common words
If your new password contains @ or #, confirm the correct keyboard layout before saving it. This helps avoid future login failures or account lockouts.

3. Set Up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Before starting, install the Microsoft Authenticator app on your mobile phone.

1
Open https://mysignins.microsoft.com/security-info
2
Sign in using your company email and your new password.
3
Click Add sign-in method.
4
Select Authenticator App.
5
Open Microsoft Authenticator on your phone and select Add account → Work or school account.
6
Scan the QR code shown on your laptop screen.
7
Approve the test notification to complete MFA setup.
Open My Sign-Ins

Microsoft Teams

  • Open Teams
  • Confirm you are signed in
  • Check chats and channels appear
If you see: "Your organization is using a newer version of Teams", contact IT so we can install the latest version.

Outlook

  • Open Outlook
  • Sign in using your company email
  • Wait for mailbox setup
  • Confirm emails start syncing

OneDrive

  • Confirm OneDrive is signed in
  • Check the sync icon appears
  • Click Save changes when prompted for backup

Open Settings.
