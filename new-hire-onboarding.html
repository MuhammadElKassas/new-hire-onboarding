01

First Login

Use the credentials on your laptop sticker

Before typing your password, make sure you're on the correct keyboard layout. Special characters behave differently on UK vs US keyboards.

CharacterUK KeyboardUS Keyboard
@ Shift + ' Shift + 2
# key near Enter Shift + 3
Use the username and temporary password printed on the sticker attached to your laptop.

🔒 Stuck on First Login?

Common issues: wrong keyboard layout, caps lock on, or sticker hard to read. Do not retry more than 3 times — your account may lock.

02

Change Your Password

You'll be prompted right after first login

Your new password must include:

At least 12 characters
Uppercase letter (A–Z)
Lowercase letter (a–z)
A number (0–9)
A special character

Avoid using:

Your first name Your surname Company name Common words
If your new password contains @ or #, confirm the correct keyboard layout before saving. Wrong layout = future lockout.

🔑 Stuck on Password?

If the password doesn't meet requirements or you're getting an error, contact IT. Do not attempt this many times or your account will lock.

03

Set Up MFA

Multi-Factor Authentication via Microsoft Authenticator

First, install Microsoft Authenticator on your mobile phone, then follow these steps on your laptop:

  1. Open mysignins.microsoft.com/security-info
  2. Sign in with your company email and new password
  3. Click Add sign-in method
  4. Select Authenticator App
  5. On your phone → Add account → Work or school account
  6. Scan the QR code shown on your laptop screen
  7. Approve the test notification to complete setup ✓
Open My Sign-Ins

📱 Stuck on MFA?

Make sure you have Microsoft Authenticator installed on your phone first. If the QR scan fails or you're not receiving the test notification, contact IT.

04

Application Checks

Verify Teams, Outlook, and OneDrive are working

💬

Microsoft Teams

  • Open Teams
  • Confirm you're signed in
  • Check chats & channels load
📧

Outlook

  • Open Outlook
  • Sign in with company email
  • Confirm emails start syncing
☁️

OneDrive

  • Confirm signed in
  • Check sync icon appears
  • Click Save changes for backup
If Teams says "Your organization is using a newer version" — contact IT for the latest version.

💻 App not working?

If any app won't open, won't sign in, or isn't syncing — note which one and contact IT. Don't spend more than 5 minutes troubleshooting on your own.

05

Windows Updates

Install all required updates before you start working

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Windows Update
  3. Click Check for updates and install all available updates
Updates may require a restart. Save any open work first.

🔄 Update issues?

If Windows Update shows an error or is stuck downloading, contact IT before trying anything else.

06

Keyboard Layout

Add US keyboard layout if preferred

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Time & Language → Language & Region
  3. Click Add a language → install English (United States)

Switch between layouts using Ctrl + Shift or click the language icon in the taskbar.

⌨️ Keyboard issues?

If you can't find the language settings or the layout isn't switching correctly, contact IT.

07

Set Timezone to Cairo

Ensures meetings & notifications show the correct local time

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Time & Language → Date & Time
  3. Under Time Zone, select (UTC+02:00) Cairo

🕐 Timezone not updating?

If the timezone option is greyed out or Cairo doesn't appear, contact IT.
